Research

We are conducting research on computer vision, machine learning, applied deep learning and multimedia related research problems. In particular, we are interested in image understanding (image parsing, image re-targeting, image re-attentionizing), visual fixation analysis with image, video, audio, depth, touch behavior; salient object detection; action/gesture recognition; human biometrics (human attractiveness, human weight prediction); augmented reality and virtual reality; and deep learning. In short, we like doing something cool.
